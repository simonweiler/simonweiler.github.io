Research Interests

My research investigates the structural and functional organization of cortical brain circuits, with a focus on multimodal sensory intra- and interhemispheric integration.

I utilize advanced techniques including in vivo and in vitro recordings, optogenetics, and structural imaging to map the connectivity and logic of the mammalian visual and somatosensory systems.
