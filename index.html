Research Interests

My research investigates the structural and functional organization of cortical circuits, with a focus on sensory integration, feedback mechanisms, and the relationship between dendritic morphology and neural tuning.

I utilize advanced techniques including in vivo and in vitro recordings, optogenetics, and structural imaging to map the connectivity and logic of the mouse visual and somatosensory systems.
